Welcome to Ka-Fei House

Café and Eatery

Our Menu Contact Us!

Ka-Fei House is a café that focuses on mouth-watering food and divine coffee.

Ka-Fei house is a new modern coffee house located in Queensbury Court, Queensbury, specialising in great coffee, food & cakes in a warm, welcoming environment.

  • We have free Wi-Fi for all of our guests.
  • Vegan and vegetarian options are available for those with dietry requirments.
  • Frendly staff will great you at the counter following excellent food service.

Our customers have a great time dining and chatting in our hospitable environment. We have great spots to take instagram posts to share with your friends. The good lighting will extenuate your better side a little further.
